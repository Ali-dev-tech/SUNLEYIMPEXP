WHO ARE WE

Welcome to Silverman Import and Export Co Limited, a leading trading company based in Hong Kong. We specialize in a diverse range of trading activities, offering top-quality products and services across multiple sectors. Our extensive portfolio includes:

• Food Stuff: We source and supply a wide variety of food products, ensuring freshness, quality, and competitive pricing.

• Textiles: Our textile division offers a broad selection of fabrics and garments, catering to both domestic and international markets.

• Auto Parts and Tyres: We provide an extensive range of auto parts and tyres, catering to various vehicle types and ensuring optimal performance and safety.

• Heavy Machinery and Equipment: Our inventory includes state-of-the-art heavy machinery and equipment, supporting various industrial needs and projects.

• Building Materials: We supply high-quality building materials to meet the demands of construction projects of all sizes.
